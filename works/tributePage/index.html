Muhammad Ali's Carrer
  • Cassius Marcellus Clay Jr, born in 1942 17 January, Louisville Kentucky.
  • Wins gold in the light heavyweight boxing division at the Rome Olympics in 1960 .
  • Wins world heavyweight titlein 1964, beating champion Sonny Liston in six rounds.
  • In 1974 regains his world title by beating the fearsome George Foreman in the”Rumble in the Jungle” in Kinshasa, Zaire. Ali wins by a knockout in the eighth round.
  • Lights the flame to begin the Olympics Games in 1996, Atlanta.
  • In 2012 he is among a number of dignitaries and other special guests to escort the Olympic flag during the 2012 Summer Olympic Games in Stratford, east London.
  • 2016 3 June dies in Phoenix, Arizona, at the age of 74.
